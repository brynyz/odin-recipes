Hungarian Sausage Sandwich

Description

A flavorful sandwich with spicy Hungarian sausage and fresh toppings in a crispy roll.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Hungarian sausage
  • 1 fresh baguette or crusty roll
  • 1 tbsp butter or mustard (optional)
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 2-3 slices of pickles
  • Fresh lettuce or arugula
  • Paprika (optional for extra flavor)

Steps

  1. Slice the baguette or roll in half and lightly toast if desired.
  2. Spread butter or mustard on one side of the bread
  3. Heat the sausage slices in a pan until slightly cripsy (optional)
  4. Layer the sausage, onion, tomato, pickles and lettuce inside the bread.
  5. Sprinkle a bit of paprika for extra flavor.
  6. Close the sandwich, slice in half, and enjoy!
